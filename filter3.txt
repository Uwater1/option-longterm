Top 5 Recommended Distinct Filters based on extensive testing:

To avoid overfitting, we tested diverse combinations of indicators including momentum (MACD, ROC), volatility/channels (Bollinger Bands, Keltner, ATR), trend (ADX, EMA crosses), and mean-reversion (RSI, Stochastics).

The ideal filter should:
1. Have a low Expected Return for filtered-out options (meaning it successfully avoids bad trades). Ideally < 1000.
2. Control max drawdown (maintain a high avg max loss compared to baseline -2097).
3. Do short call options in >70% of time (placement rate >= 70%).

Here are the top 5 distinct filters meeting these criteria:

1. RSI < 66 AND Close < Upper Bollinger Band (20, 2)
   - Placement Rate: 88.0% (66/75 cycles)
   - Avg Max Loss: -492.57 RMB
   - Avg EV (Included): 1542.69 RMB
   - Avg EV (Filtered Out): 637.51 RMB
   - Logic: A classic mean-reversion + volatility band combination. Highly effective at capturing safe trades while cutting off tail risk. Filtered-out cycles are extremely poor performers.

2. EMA10 < EMA30 OR RSI < 60
   - Placement Rate: 81.3% (61/75 cycles)
   - Avg Max Loss: -492.57 RMB
   - Avg EV (Included): 1549.61 RMB
   - Avg EV (Filtered Out): 931.29 RMB
   - Logic: Combines trend alignment (fast EMA < slow EMA) with a stricter mean-reversion boundary. We sell calls when there is a downtrend OR when it's not remotely overbought.

3. MACD Hist < 0 OR ADX < 25
   - Placement Rate: 84.0% (63/75 cycles)
   - Avg Max Loss: -1814.75 RMB
   - Avg EV (Included): 1532.79 RMB
   - Avg EV (Filtered Out): 914.64 RMB
   - Logic: Momentum + Trend strength. Sells calls when momentum is negative OR when the overall trend is weak (low ADX). Has a slightly worse max drawdown but excellently isolates poor EVs in the rejected bucket.

4. RSI < 60 AND Close < Keltner Channel (EMA20 + 1*ATR)
   - Placement Rate: 77.3% (58/75 cycles)
   - Avg Max Loss: -188.18 RMB
   - Avg EV (Included): 1589.77 RMB
   - Avg EV (Filtered Out): 985.37 RMB
   - Logic: Uses Keltner Channels to identify resistance combined with RSI not being overbought. Exceptionally strong max drawdown protection while keeping filtered out EV below 1000.

5. RSI < 62 AND Close < Upper Bollinger Band (20, 2)
   - Placement Rate: 85.3% (64/75 cycles)
   - Avg Max Loss: -188.18 RMB
   - Avg EV (Included): 1541.69 RMB
   - Avg EV (Filtered Out): 926.38 RMB
   - Logic: Similar to #1 but with a tighter RSI constraint. Provides excellent drawdown protection while still keeping the placement rate above 85% and the filtered out EV below 1000.
